Milestones

  1. Initiate
  2. Connect
  3. Grow
  4. Transition

Initiate

The first phase in the mentorship lifecycle starts with the first step you take towards finding a mentor or mentee. This phase represents you making an account with Mentch to start your mentorship process.

Connect

The next phase in your mentorship journey is connecting with your mentor or mentee. This happens when you are matched according to our initial survey in our system and once the mentee accepts a mentor the connection is formed.

Grow

The third milestone occurs once either a mentor or mentee reaches out to chat with their mentor/mentee. Once you have had your first conversation in our chat section of the website you will reach this milestone.

Transition

Our final step in the mentorship lifecycle is the transitional phase. This happens when you have been mentored and feel comfortable enough in your role and experiences to take on a mentee of your own. This milestone will be complete once you change your status from a mentee to a mentor.
